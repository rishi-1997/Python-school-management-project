WELCOME TO ST. JHON EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY

The St. Jhon Educational Society (J.E.S.), a charitable institution registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860, is engaged in educational, social, developmental and charitable activities as embodied in the aims and objectives of the Society. Our endeavor is furthering quality education through a chain of schools and other educational facilities. J.E.S. was formed with the objective to establish new vistas in the areas of education and focuses on the all round development of an individual. “In the Forefront of Nation Building” is our mission and our aim is to achieve new Horizons in education.Through meaningful programs and projects, J.E.S translates the above aim into a reality.
